How to Play

Getting Started Guide

1

Download and Install

Download the installer from the "Download" button on the top page and run it. Simply follow the on-screen instructions — the KataGo AI engine will be automatically installed along with the app.

On the first install, a Windows SmartScreen warning may appear. Click "More info" → "Run anyway" to proceed.
2

Create an Account

A login screen will appear on the first launch. Click the "Register" button to create an account. Enter your name, handle name (displayed during matches), email address, password, and skill level.

Handle names must be 20 characters or less. Skill level ranges from 20-kyu to 9-dan.
3

Log In

Log in with the email address and password you registered. After logging in, the main screen (Go board) will be displayed.

4

Start a Match

There are two ways to play a match.

• AI Match — Select "AI Match" from the menu. Choose from 30 levels of KataGo as your opponent. Perfect for solo practice.

• Online Match — Send a challenge to other logged-in players, or accept a challenge from another player. Play against players from around the world in real time.

5

Game Rules

Goka GO uses Chinese rules. The default komi is 7.5 points. During the game, a real-time win rate evaluation (%) is displayed so you can gauge your advantage or disadvantage.

6

Time Controls and Byo-yomi

Each match has a main time setting. When the main time runs out, byo-yomi (countdown) begins. During byo-yomi, a voice countdown will play. If you don't make a move within the time limit, you lose on time.

7

Game End and Review

When both players pass, the territory is calculated and the result is displayed. After the game, you can review the match in review mode. Use the navigation bar to step forward or backward through the moves.
