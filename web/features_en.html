Features

What Goka GO Offers

🤖

Built-in KataGo AI Engine

Powered by KataGo, one of the world's strongest open-source Go AIs. With 30 difficulty levels, players of all skill levels — from beginners to advanced — can find a suitable opponent. During gameplay, a real-time win rate evaluation (%) is displayed so you can instantly see how well you are playing.

🌐

Online Matches

Play against players from around the world in real time over the internet. Send and receive challenges, with match request timeout management for a smooth experience.

📊

Elo Rating

Your Elo rating is automatically calculated based on match results. Track your skill level numerically and watch your improvement over time.

🔍

KataGo Scoring

When both players pass, KataGo accurately calculates the territory. The AI handles dead stone determination, so even beginners can end games with confidence.

📋

Game Review Mode

After a game ends, step through the moves forward and backward using the navigation bar. See where the balance shifted and learn for your next game.

⏱️

Time Controls & Byo-yomi

Supports main time and byo-yomi (countdown). A voice countdown plays during byo-yomi. Voice countdown is available in 4 languages: Japanese, English, Chinese, and Korean.

🌏

Multilingual Support

Supports 4 languages: Japanese, English, Chinese, and Korean. Switch the UI language with a single click.

🔄

Auto-Update

New versions are automatically detected when the app launches. Update with one click to always stay on the latest version.
